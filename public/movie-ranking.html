Andrew's Best Movies


My Top 3 Movies of All Time


Strange Brew

An old odd-ball comedy about two brothers trying to scam a brewery for free beer but accidentally stumble upon a plot by the head brewer to create a mind control beer. Silly and over the top, the type of comedy to turn your brain off and grab some popcorn.


The Brothers Bloom

Two orphaned brothers learn to run cons at an early age and survive by conning their way through life. The elder brother wants to write the perfect con while the younger brother wants to get out of the con life. A few twists and turns make this an entertaining and light-hearted comedy/drama that entertains throughout.


Bullet Train

A true popcorn flick. Doesn't make you think and keeps you entertained with consistent action. Easy to follow and looks great on screen. Good performances throughout.
